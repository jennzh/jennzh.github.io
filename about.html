about me

Hi, I'm Jennifer! I'm passionate about bridging the gap between technology and human experience through thoughtful design and development.

As an Informatics student specializing in Human-Computer Interaction, I'm driven by the belief that technology should feel natural, accessible, and empowering for everyone.

My experience spans from developing educational games with NASA to designing e-commerce solutions and conducting user research. I thrive at the intersection of technical implementation and user-centered design, whether I'm coding, prototyping, or architecting cloud solutions on AWS.

What excites me most is solving complex problems through innovative technology while keeping the human element at the forefront. I'm always eager to learn, collaborate, and create solutions that make a meaningful impact.

When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me exploring new matcha cafes, beating Candy Crush levels, or staying active through running and pickleball.
