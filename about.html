ABOUT ME


Hi! My name is Jennifer, and I am excited to share my academic portfolio with you. I am a passionate developer based in Irvine, CA with a background in Informatics and a specialization in Human Computer Interaction from UCI. With extensive experience in front-end and back-end developing, I specialize in Python, C++. and ML.

What truly interests me is the intersection of technology and human interaction. I’m driven by a desire to solve problems and create user-friendly applications that make people's lives easier. Whether it’s collaborating with a team or diving deep into solo projects, I thrive in dynamic environments where I can tackle challenges, learn, and grow.

Outside of tech, I’m an avid reader and love staying active through running and playing pickleball. Recently, I’ve also started learning how to modify my car—a fun project that fuels my curiosity and love for learning new things. I believe that a passion for continuous learning is key to thriving in any field.

Thanks for stopping by, and I’d love to connect and hear your thoughts or ideas!
